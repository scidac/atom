DIII-D #170873

Description

This H-mode discharge was part of an experiment designed to investigate various microinstabilities responsible for saturating the pedestal and investigate triggering mechanisms of type-I ELMs. This disharge has a NBI power of 2.4MW and 1.8MW of ECH. The major plasma parameters are Bt=2.1T, Ip=0.99MA, kappa=1.76, and delta(u,l)=(0.24,0.44).
