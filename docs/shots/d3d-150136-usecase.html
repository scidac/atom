DIII-D #150136

Description

This L-mode discharge was part of a shortfall experiment where the plasma current scan was varied at fixed toroidal field and plasma shape. It was heated with 2.3W of NBI. Analysis was performed at 2.086 secs.

The major plasma parameters are Bt=1.9T, Ip=1.4MA, kappa=1.77, and delta(u,l)=(0.67,0.31). This discharge complements the other discharges in this series at 0.8 and 1.0MA.
