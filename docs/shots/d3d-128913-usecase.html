DIII-D #128913

Description

This L-mode discharge was part of an L-mode experiment designed to be steady-state, MHD free, sawtooth free, and utilize synthetic diagnostics for comparison with local gyrokinetic simulations. It was heated with 2.6W of NBI. Analysis was performed at 1.50 secs.

The major plasma parameters are Bt=2.1T, Ip=1.0MA, kappa=1.65, and delta(u,l)=(0.67,0.31).
